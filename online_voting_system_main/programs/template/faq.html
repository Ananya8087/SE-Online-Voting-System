Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) - Online Voting

1. Is online voting secure?

Online voting systems are designed with security measures to protect the integrity and confidentiality of votes. These systems often use encryption and authentication to ensure a secure voting process.

2. How can I register for online voting?

You can visit our home page and register. Make sure to provide accurate information to complete the registration successfully.

3. What devices can I use for online voting?

It is recommended to use a secure and up-to-date device, such as a personal computer or a smartphone, with antivirus software. Avoid using public computers or unsecured networks to reduce the risk of unauthorized access.

4. Can I change my vote after submitting it online?

Yes! We allow the voter to actively change votes until the last second of the election.

5. Is my personal information safe during online voting?

Online voting systems prioritize the security of voter information. Follow best practices such as keeping your login credentials confidential and using secure devices to enhance the safety of your personal information.

6. What should I do if I encounter technical issues during online voting?

If you experience technical difficulties, such as connectivity issues or system errors, contact the election authorities immediately for assistance. They can provide guidance and support to resolve the issues.
